About

I am about to be a Junior in college on a path to become a Secondary Education Math Teacher. I simply have a passion for education and children. I found my passion at a very early age, for as soon as I could write, I knew I wanted to be a teacher. I have a large family including many younger siblings and cousins which I helped raise and this is where my experience started. I would play school with my younger siblings any free time we had, and they learned much from that time. With my natural motherly instinct, I have lots of experience dealing with children outside of my family as well. I have worked with the Special Needs community, and also 1st grade through 8th grade in a school setting. I love what I do, and find great happiness in my work.

Secondary Education Teacher

In my classroom, I want to teach with my heart and build a family that grows and learns together. But,I plan to go beyond teaching in a classroom, as much as I would enjoy it. I want to change the school systems and Common Core. I want to educate not only students but, parents, other teachers, and so much more. I feel that education has strayed away from teaching for that sole purpose-- to teach and to change a life. The school system has lost its fire within its curriculum, and so have many teachers and this reflects in our students. I believe that as I move up in the world of Education, I can change many lives. Starting with my own classroom, to an entire school, to an entire county of schools, and hopefully, one day, the whole Education System in the United States. It all starts with one teacher to make that difference in a students life, and that difference can be the thing that helps that student become the next President, the next Teacher of the Year, or the next Scientist that cures cancer (or COVID). Its important to value our students because they are our upcoming generations and they need and deserve amazing, dedicated, caring, whole-hearted teachers to help them and guide them to be their best selves. That starts with us! It starts with me!

Teaching Philosophies

In my classroom, I want to focus on the learning environment. I believe that students learn best, and obtain knowledge when they are in a comfortable learning environment. Students should feel loved and accepted in each classroom they walk into, and I can assure you, that students will in my classroom. If a student feels loved, understood, and accepted as who they are then they can come to you openly about educational questions and sometimes even life questions. Students will be learning the materials better this way, and be learning more about life. This allows the students to be free of everything when they walk into the classroom as well, so that in their minds they can have room to learn and learn happily. My classroom will be a family. My classroom will always be a safe spot to learn and find yourself. It will be a space students can come be themselves and learn whichever way they need to, to be their best selves. Their best selves, in my classroom and beyond. It all starts with an effective and loving learning environment.
